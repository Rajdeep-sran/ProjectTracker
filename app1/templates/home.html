About ProjectTracker

A productivity platform crafted for students and teams to visually track, manage, and complete projects efficiently.

Visual Overview of Projects

Stay on top of your progress with insightful graphs. The pie chart shows the distribution of your projects by status, while the bar graph highlights how many days remain for each active project.

Whether you're managing one project or ten, these visuals help you prioritize and act swiftly.

Smart Milestone Tracking

Break projects into clear, actionable milestones. Each task has its own due date, priority, and completion status – all tracked in a clean, sortable table.

Easily monitor what’s done, what’s pending, and what’s urgent. Stay deadline-focused and never miss a critical task.

All-in-One Dashboard

Your personalized dashboard gives a quick glance at project progress, task urgency, and upcoming deadlines—all in one place.

It's built for simplicity and clarity so you can make confident decisions at a glance and stay organized effortlessly.
